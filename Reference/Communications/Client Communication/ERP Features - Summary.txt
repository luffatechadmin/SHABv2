a centralized operational brain (ERP + intelligence layer) that gives real-time control across the business.

To design this properly, think in functional domains, not just features. Below is a complete coverage model for a one-stop dashboard tailored to a cosmetics OEM manufacturer.

🧠 1. Executive Control Tower (Top-Level Dashboard)

This is what makes your system powerful.

Should show:
Daily revenue, orders, production status
Inventory health (low stock, expiring items)
Cash flow snapshot
Workforce status (attendance, productivity)
Alerts & exceptions (delays, shortages, overdue payments)

👉 This is NOT just charts — it’s a decision-making cockpit.

🏭 2. Production & Manufacturing Management

Core for OEM cosmetics.

Must include:
Production planning & scheduling
Work orders
Bill of Materials (BOM)
Batch production tracking
WIP (Work-in-progress) monitoring
Yield & wastage tracking
Advanced (high value):
Capacity planning
Machine/lab utilization
Production cost per batch
📦 3. Inventory & Warehouse Management

Highly critical in cosmetics due to expiry & compliance.

Coverage:
Raw materials, packaging, finished goods
Batch & lot tracking
Expiry date monitoring
Multi-location warehouse support
Stock movement history
Smart features:
Auto stock alerts
FIFO/FEFO logic (expiry-based usage)
Barcode / QR integration
💰 4. Sales & Order Management (OEM-focused)

Not just simple sales — this is B2B contract manufacturing.

Must include:
Quotation management
Sales order tracking
Custom pricing per client
MOQ handling
Delivery scheduling
Add intelligence:
Sales pipeline visibility
Client profitability analysis
Repeat order prediction
🧾 5. Accounting & Financial Management

The system must become the single source of truth for money.

Core:
General ledger
Accounts receivable / payable
Invoicing
Payment tracking
Manufacturing-specific:
Cost of goods manufactured (COGM)
Margin per product / client
Budget vs actual tracking
👥 6. Human Resource (HR) & Workforce Management

Essential for operational visibility.

Include:
Attendance tracking
Leave management
Payroll (salary, OT, deductions)
Employee performance tracking
Optional upgrade:
Productivity tracking (linked to production output)
🔗 7. Procurement & Supplier Management

Often forgotten, but critical.

Must include:
Purchase requisitions
Purchase orders
Supplier database
Material lead time tracking
Smart layer:
Supplier performance (price, quality, delivery)
Cost trend analysis
🧪 8. Quality Control & Compliance

Very important for cosmetics manufacturing.

Coverage:
QC checkpoints (incoming, in-process, finished goods)
Lab testing records
Batch approval/rejection
Audit logs
Compliance-related:
GMP documentation
Product traceability (for recalls)
📊 9. Business Intelligence & Reporting Layer

This is what separates a normal system from a powerful one.

Should provide:
Cross-department insights
Custom report builder
KPI tracking per department
Examples:
Profit per batch
Inventory turnover rate
Production efficiency
Sales vs capacity
🔔 10. Alerts, Notifications & Exception Handling

This is where real operational value comes in.

System should automatically alert:
Low or expiring stock
Delayed production orders
Late payments
QC failures
Attendance anomalies

👉 This turns the system from passive → proactive.

🔐 11. Role-Based Access & Workflow Engine

Critical for control and accountability.

Include:
Role-based dashboards (Admin, Manager, Staff)
Approval workflows:
Purchases
Payments
Production release
Activity logs (who did what)
🔄 12. Integration Layer (VERY IMPORTANT)

Since client currently uses multiple systems.

Your system should:
Replace OR integrate with:
Accounting software
POS / sales tools
Payment gateways
Excel imports (initial phase)
Future-proof:
API-ready architecture
📱 13. Mobility & Accessibility

Modern expectation.

Include:
Mobile-friendly dashboard
Role-based mobile views
Real-time updates
🧩 14. Master Data & Configuration Layer

The backbone of flexibility.

Should manage:
Products & formulations
Pricing rules
Tax settings
Workflow rules
Units & categories
🧠 15. Automation & Smart Features (Future Advantage)

This is where you differentiate.

Potential features:
Auto production planning based on orders
Demand forecasting
Smart reorder suggestions
Cost optimization insights
🔥 How to Think About This System (Key Insight)

You are not building:

“Inventory + Sales + HR system”

You are building:

An integrated operational ecosystem where every module feeds each other in real time
